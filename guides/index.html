  • 2

Purchase a Website

MyanCode offers two powerful ways to acquire a professional website, depending on your business requirements.

Method 1: Custom Tailored Website

Build exactly what you need with dynamic, customized pricing.

  • 1

    Desktop: Click "Studio" from the main header.
    Mobile: Tap the Menu icon, then select "Studio".

  • 2

    In the Studio interface, browse and add your desired custom designs, advanced functions, and total page count.

  • 3

    Submit your tailored request and our engineering team will handle the rest.

Method 2: Ready-Made Templates

Purchase complete, premium codebases that you can adjust and own entirely.

  • 1

    Desktop: Click "Templates" from the main header.
    Mobile: Tap the Menu icon, then select "Templates".

  • 2

    Browse our catalog, select the template that fits your needs, and click Purchase to proceed to checkout.

Method 3: Professional Services

Request specific web development and custom services directly.

  • 1

    Navigate to the Services page from the main header or mobile menu.

  • 2

    Select your desired service, view the detailed description, and click the Purchase or Request button.
